Privacy Policy for Sree Guru Sannidhi

Last Updated: April 23, 2026

SGS Bookings ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Sree Guru Sannidhi mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle user information, particularly concerning our integrated web-based booking system.

1. Information We Collect

To provide booking services, our app displays an integrated web interface where users can book rooms. Through this interface, we may collect:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, email address, phone number, and physical address.
  • Financial Information: Credit/debit card numbers and billing information. Payments are processed securely via our integrated payment gateway; we do not store full credit card details on our local app servers.
  • Booking History: Details regarding the rooms and dates you have reserved.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used strictly for processing reservations, handling payments, and providing customer support.

3. Third-Party Services & WebView

Our app uses a WebView to display our booking website. Information is transmitted securely to our servers and PCI-compliant payment processors. Our app also uses Google Play Services, which may collect device identification data.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard encryption (SSL/HTTPS) to protect all data entered into the booking portal.

5. Your Rights

Users have the right to request the deletion of their personal data. To do so, please contact us at the email below.
